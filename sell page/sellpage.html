Why choose Auctions Table as your disposal partner?

At Auctions Table, we recognise that every business has unique requirements when it comes to asset disposal. Whether you need to create space quickly or generate revenue fast, we can work with you to create a disposal solution to suit your specific needs.

Experts in commercial asset disposal.

Our team of experts will work closely with you to understand specific requirements of your organization & devise a strategy.

Whether you prefer to sell from your own site or utilise our Northern warehouse, we have flexibility to accommodate your preferences.

Our team has over half a century of combined experience and knowledge in the asset disposal industry, making us a trusted partner for businesses seeking an effective solution. We specialise in the disposal stage of the asset lifecycle, offering solutions for every scenario - from the removal of single machines to full site clearances.

Our extensive database of thousands of engaged and interested buyers and bespoke marketing strategy allows us to create the best sale experience for our clients, ensuring maximum returns for their assets. You can rely on our expertise to provide advice on reserves, cataloguing, photography, and item descriptions, optimising the visibility and appeal of your items for sale.

The AuctionTable industry-leading timed auction platform offers a seamless experience for buyers and sellers alike. Our user-friendly interface allows buyers to easily browse and purchase commercial assets, while sellers benefit from the AuctionTable simple five-step selling process.

By leveraging intelligent auction technology, our platform presents your sale to an extensive buyer audience, achieving higher returns for your items.

Many businesses are unaware of the value of assets they have and no longer require. Our team can provide valuable insights into the realistic worth of your assets and offer tailored disposal solutions to meet your individual needs.

Whether you need to sell, repurpose, recycle, or dispose of your assets, we have the expertise to guide you through the process. Partnering with AuctionTable allows you to uncover the hidden value in your assets and turn them into tangible returns.

We understand that dealing with asset disposal is often a low priority for businesses. However, by partnering with AuctionTable, you can accelerate the disposal process and achieve maximum returns for your equipment. Our tailored marketing campaigns and online platform enable us to present your assets to thousands of individuals, attracting the perfect buyers for your sale.

Unlike traditional routes that can take months to complete, our streamlined approach significantly reduces the turnaround time. On average, the AuctionTable selling process is completed within 4-5 weeks, meaning you will be paid for your asset disposal in less than 6 weeks.

In today’s world, sustainability has become a crucial focus for businesses. AuctionTable can help your organisation contribute to the circular economy by repurposing surplus machinery or equipment. Our focus on sustainability aligns with your business goals and contributes to the circular economy.

As large corporations regularly renew and upgrade their fleets, surplus machinery needs to be removed responsibly. By partnering with AuctionTable, you can fulfil your environmental targets and comply with commercial waste regulations, ensuring a sustainable approach to asset disposal.

Our tailored solutions, backed by a wealth of industry experience, ensure that your unique needs are met effectively. With our user-friendly auction platform and bespoke marketing campaigns, we drive maximum returns for your assets.
