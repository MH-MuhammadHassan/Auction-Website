Who is Auctions Table?

Founded in 2010, Auctions Table exists to help businesses buy & sell commercial assets in the smartest way possible. Whether you're starting up or retiring; relocating or restructuring; scaling or closing, we can help you to buy & sell assets quickly & efficiently.

Fast, hassle-free commercial asset disposal

We manage the complete selling process from pre-sale cataloguing & valuations to post-sale payments & collections, taking the hassle out of selling for you.

Connecting buyers
& sellers together.
It's what we do.

We tailor disposal solutions for businesses, large or small, helping them realise the value of assets, create space, & generate revenue.

Straight talking. Deal making. Market disrupters.

We are known for our knowledge, trusted for our straight-talking approach, & preferred by many businesses for our innovative, & industry-leading timed auction platform.

Sell with Auctions Table

Auctions Table exists to help businesses buy & sell industry items & is known for our expertise across multiple sectors. From small independents to multinational corporations, Auctions Table can help you realise the value of your assets, create space, & generate revenue.

Buy with Auctions Table

It's easy to bid with Auctions Table. We offer a broad selection of items spread across multiple categories, which can be bid on through our industry-leading, timed auction platform.
